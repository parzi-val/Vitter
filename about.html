Welcome to Vitter!
We are a social media web-platform that connects people exclusively from VIT. Our mission is to create a safe, inclusive and fun platform for VITians. This platform was created by a team of passionate developers who saw a need for a judgement and harassment free social media platform just for VITians to connect among themselves whilst prioritizing user privacy and security. With Vitter, one can connect with friends and colleagues, meet new people with shared interests, and discover new communities. If you have any feedback or suggestions, feel free to contact us.
Thank You for choosing Vitter! We can't wait to see how you use it to grow your network.
